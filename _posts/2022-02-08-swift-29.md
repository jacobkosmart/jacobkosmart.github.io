## 🔷

```swift
let myFriends = ["Jacob", "Emma", "Jason"]
let otherFriends = ["Mike", "Luck", "Paul"]

// + 더하기를 통해 collection 을 합칠 수 있습니다
let totalFriends = myFriends + otherFriends
print(totalFriends) // ["Jacob", "Emma", "Jason", "Mike", "Luck", "Paul"]
```
